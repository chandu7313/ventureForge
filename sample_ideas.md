## 1. FinTech / AgriTech: KrishiPay

**Startup Name:**
KrishiPay

**The Problem & Solution:**
Indian farmers lack access to instant, low-interest micro-credit during the sowing season and often rely on predatory local lenders. We are building a unified UPI-based lending platform that uses alternative data (crop satellite imagery, historical weather data, and past mandi sales records) to dynamically underwrite and disburse micro-loans instantly to farmers' bank accounts without requiring traditional collateral.

**Target Audience:**
Small and marginal farmers in tier-3 cities and rural India who own less than 2 hectares of land.

**Industry/Sector:**
FinTech / AgriTech

**Target Geography:**
PAN_INDIA

**Current Stage:**
prototype

**Team Size:**
3

**Primary Skill of Founding Team:**
Software Engineering & Rural Banking

**Initial Budget / Funding:**
< ₹5L

## 2. EdTech: SkillBharat

**Startup Name:**
SkillBharat

**The Problem & Solution:**
Millions of graduates in tier-2 and tier-3 Indian cities are unemployable because existing tech bootcamps are in English and priced for the urban elite. SkillBharat provides cohort-based, AI-assisted tech upskilling (MERN stack, Data Science) entirely in regional languages (Hindi, Tamil, Telugu). We use AI tutors for 24/7 doubt resolution and offer Income Share Agreements (ISAs) to remove upfront friction.

**Target Audience:**
Tier-2/3 college students and fresh graduates looking for entry-level tech jobs.

**Industry/Sector:**
EdTech

**Target Geography:**
PAN_INDIA

**Current Stage:**
idea

**Team Size:**
2

**Primary Skill of Founding Team:**
Curriculum Design & EdTech Operations

**Initial Budget / Funding:**
₹5L - ₹10L

## 3. HealthTech: MediSync AI

**Startup Name:**
MediSync AI

**The Problem & Solution:**
Rural and semi-urban Primary Health Centres (PHCs) in India are severely understaffed. Doctors spend less than 2 minutes per patient. MediSync AI provides an offline-first voice assistant in local dialects that interviews the patient while they wait, generates a structured triage report, and suggests a preliminary differential diagnosis for the doctor. This reduces consultation time by 60% and improves diagnostic accuracy.

**Target Audience:**
Government Primary Health Centres, rural NGO clinics, and affordable private clinic chains.

**Industry/Sector:**
HealthTech / AI

## 4. PropTech / Legal: ClearTitle AI

**Startup Name:**
ClearTitle AI

**The Problem & Solution:**
Real estate transactions in India are plagued by opaque property histories, resulting in massive litigation over disputed titles. ClearTitle AI uses computer vision and LLMs to digitize and parse decades of handwritten, regional-language property records (7/12 extracts, encumbrance certificates). It instantly flags legal risks, missing links in the chain of ownership, and outstanding liens, cutting down legal due diligence from weeks to minutes.

**Target Audience:**
Real estate developers, NBFCs, housing finance companies, and retail property buyers.

**Industry/Sector:**
PropTech / LegalTech

**Target Geography:**
CITY_LOCAL

**Current Stage:**
revenue

**Team Size:**
5

**Primary Skill of Founding Team:**
Corporate Law & Computer Vision (AI)

**Initial Budget / Funding:**
> ₹50L

## 5. Logistics / DeepTech: OptiRoute EV

**Startup Name:**
OptiRoute EV

**The Problem & Solution:**
Commercial EV fleets (last-mile delivery) suffer from "range anxiety" and highly inefficient charging schedules, leading to vehicle downtime and lost revenue. OptiRoute EV is an intelligent fleet management SaaS. It pulls live battery telemetry and local traffic data to assign dynamic delivery routes, automatically reserving slots at partner fast-charging hubs exactly when the vehicle drops below 15% charge.

**Target Audience:**
Last-mile logistics companies (Zepto, Blinkit, Amazon), D2C brands with proprietary fleets, and third-party EV fleet operators.

**Industry/Sector:**
Logistics / DeepTech

**Target Geography:**
PAN_INDIA

**Current Stage:**
mvp

**Team Size:**
3

**Primary Skill of Founding Team:**
Supply Chain Logistics & IoT Hardware

**Initial Budget / Funding:**
₹10L - ₹50L
